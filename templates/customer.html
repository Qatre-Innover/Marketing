About Our Project

Customer Personality Analysis is a detailed analysis of a company’s ideal customers. It helps a business to better understand its customers and makes it easier for them to modify products according to the specific needs, behaviors and concerns of different types of customers. Where large MNCs are using this criteria very frequently, we aim to provide this benefit to every small business.

For example, instead of spending money to market a new product to every customer in the company’s database, a company can analyse which customer segment is most likely to buy the product and then market the product only on that particular segment.
